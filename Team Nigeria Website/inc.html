International Collaboration

"Home is not a place, it's a feeling.
Nigeria is home"

Nigeria is a blend of thousands of cultures and over hundreds of languages, but it doesn’t matter if it’s the dancing masquerades of the Eyo Festival or the energetic fishermen during the Argungu Fishing Festival, we know it’s home.

Being members of FIRST Global Challenge’s Team Nigeria gave us the opportunity not only to meet people from around the world, it gave us the opportunity to share our home with our new friends.
